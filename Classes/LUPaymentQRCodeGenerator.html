Overview

LUPaymentQRCodeGenerator is used to build QR codes for users' payment tokens. All LevelUp QR codes include a unique identifier for the current user, but may optionally include a tip percentage or the ID of a color that the user would like the LevelUp docks to glow.

Glow Colors

The current table of colors is:

  • 0: #00FF00 “Soothing Green”
  • 1: #BAFF48 “Atomic Lime”
  • 2: #FF0002 “Tango Red”
  • 3: #0030F8 “Galactic Blue”
  • 4: #7800F7 “Purple Haze”
  • 5: #FFFC48 “Yellow Submarine”
  • 6: #00E4FD “Arctic Teal”
  • 7: #FF00C6 “Sick Flamingo”
  • 8: #00FF84 “Mint Julep”
  • 9: #FF721C “Ninja Orange”
